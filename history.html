...We Bang
History Lesson:

What started as an experiment to help fill the time between costume changes in a local musical has turned into widely successful Petting Zoo. The trio, having formerly jammed together as percussionists at the local high school, quickly became popular in their local Nevada County community.
Since that time, Petting Zoo has gone on to perform all over Northern California and even have starred in their own commercial for a regional WB televison affiliate.
